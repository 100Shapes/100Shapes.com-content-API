What is interactive video advertising?

Interactive videos create immersive, user-driven experiences that link content with marketing and commerce.

Interactive video advertising blends video content with user interaction. Be it gestures, voice, touch or clicks — the richness of the user interaction can run across the spectrum.

Visual elements within a video invite viewers to click or tap to perform an action. They can be associated with items in the story, triggered by the timeline or are continually visible as an overlay to the video.

Benefits of interactive video advertising

Offering a layer of interactivity encourages audiences to engage with advertising. It gives them the chance to willingly spend more time with your brand online. There are many benefits:

Capture your audience’s attention for longer

Engaged viewers are more likely to finish watching your video content. Interactive videos can hold your audience’s attention for longer than normal, linear videos.

Give your customers the control

Hand over the reigns to your customers by letting them shape the way they consume your video content. Let them explore content and information that’s relevant to them.

Learn from your customers

Identify what makes your audience tick. Discover which content is attracting interest and where it falls down. As viewers interact with your video, use their responses to accumulate information about their behaviours and interests.

Be available on any device

With mobile video consumption on an upward trajectory, let audiences access your interactive content on a device of their choosing.

Make it memorable

With audience’s spending more time interacting with you content, the experience is more memorable and one they are more likely to share. Keeping your viewers active will hold their attention for longer, increase engagement, recall, satisfaction and time spent watching a video.
