What is an interactive screen?

Interactive display screens are giant touch-enabled displays for use from within the broadcast studio. They’re designed to be used by presenters during a programme to show dynamic multimedia content to audiences watching at home or online.

Creating a UX Strategy

Defining your user experience strategy has many parallels to how you define your business strategy. It comprises of the following steps:

A clear view of where you are

In order to take your product or service forward, you need to know where you are to begin with. We’ll kick things off by gathering information about your business and users.

Establishing your vision

Next it’s time to work out where you want to be. Armed with the information we’ve gathered, we brainstorm and prioritise strategies that have a measurable impact on your business.

Get the job done

With a clear road map of who does what and when, it’s time to roll up our sleeves and implement your vision. Our strategists and designers work with you to develop your goals into a tangible product (or service) to deliver the business outcomes you are targeting.

Time to respond

Once your project is out in the world, we’ll gather metrics and useful feedback to determine its success. Armed with these insights we’ll make any necessary improvements before tackling the next objective.

Case study

London Live

With more than 6 hours of daily live news and entertainments programming, interactive screens have transformed the way London Live’s presenters communicate with their audiences.

100 Shapes devised and designed a suite of interactive big screen applications for the London Live presenters to clearly communicate news, travel and weather content at a moments notice.

Cleverly integrated

From slideshows to interactive maps, video walls to weather reports. Easily create rich multimedia presentations at a moment’s notice.

Intuitive admin

Required to display real-time data at a moment’s notice, the interactive display showcase live news, weather and travel data with minimal set up. London Live’s presenters use an intuitive admin to quickly set up and create highly visual presentations.

London Live’s formats included:

News headlines

As text, image, video, rich multimedia, graphics and data.

Real-time social media, social analytics and audience participation from all the major networks.
